Some of my favorite things

Favorite Movie

One of my favorite movies is Knives Out. It's a fun murder mystery with a huge ensemble cast, great acting and an original storyline that felt like a breath of fresh air. A sequel was recently released, and I hope for even more entries in the series in the future.

Favorite Music

My favorite band is a folk group out of Seattle called Fleet Foxes. Their lead singer and songwriter, Robin Pecknold, is one of my favorite lyricists and their music makes me wish I was cozied up in a warm cabin in the woods somewhere.
